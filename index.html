About Me

I'm a data professional with expertise in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. My work focuses on creating interactive visualizations and data-driven stories that help organizations make informed decisions.

I started using Tableau in 2019, later transitioning to Power BI. I have used it for the last 6 years to help Quality functions for KPI and compliance reporting. This role allowed me to learn about Alteryx for data cleaning and transformations, PowerQuery (M), data modeling, and Measures and Calculated columns using DAX.

As part of my previous projects, I have worked with SQL and Snowflake to bring data into Power BI. I specialize in developing comprehensive reports that reveal hidden patterns and trends in data.
